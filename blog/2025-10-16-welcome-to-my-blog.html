Welcome to My Blog

October 16, 2025 by Catarina Gamboa

Hello and welcome to my blog! I'm thrilled to finally have a dedicated space to share my thoughts, experiences, and insights from my journey as a dual Ph.D. student in Software Engineering.

Why Start a Blog?

As researchers, we often get caught up in the technical details of our work—writing papers, conducting experiments, and perfecting our implementations. But there's so much more to the research journey that doesn't always make it into academic publications. This blog is my attempt to capture those moments: the challenges, the breakthroughs, the learning experiences, and the reflections that shape who I am as a researcher.

What to Expect

In this space, I'll be sharing:

  • Research insights: Deep dives into my work on liquid types, software verification, and programming language design
  • Academic life: Experiences navigating the dual Ph.D. program between CMU and the University of Lisbon
  • Technical tutorials: Explanations of concepts I'm working with, hopefully making them more accessible
  • Reflections: Thoughts on research, academia, and the broader impact of our work

Let's Connect

I believe that research is fundamentally a collaborative endeavor. If anything I write resonates with you, or if you have questions or thoughts to share, please don't hesitate to reach out. You can find me on GitHub and LinkedIn, or send me an email.

Thank you for joining me on this journey. Here's to many more posts ahead!

— Catarina
